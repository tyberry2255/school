Recipe #1: Cheese Its

  • Time: 1-2 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Cheddar-Jack Cheese 1 handful

Step 1: Place the cheese on a microwaveable plate.

Step 2: Microwave the cheese for 1 minute on high

Step 3: Peel the cheese off of the plate

WARNING: Peel it off slowly. Also it might be sharp, so watch out.

Step 4: Enjoy this incredibly unhealthy snack!

Recipe #2: Quesadilla

  • Time: 5-10 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Cheddar-Jack Cheese 1-2 handfuls
Tortillas (Not Corn) 2 slices

Step 1: Turn on a grittle to its maximum level.

Step 2: Place one tortilla on the grittle.

Step 3: Heavily sprinkle the cheese onto the tortilla, leaving a half inch of tortilla uncovered along the edge.

Step 4: Place your second tortilla onto the cheese, on top of the previous tortilla.

Step 5: Keep an eye on the bottom of the quesadilla, and flip when you see around three spots of dark orange-brown.

Step 6: Place on a plate when the side you flipped on is done.

WARNING: Now this is the tricky part, it only cooks in select places, and the rest of the tortilla will look the same. Make sure you do not cook it too long, or else it will burn

Step 7: Enjoy!

Recipe #3: Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwitch

  • Time: 2-3 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Peanut Butter 1-3 knife-fulls
Bread 2 slices
Jelly 1 spoonful

Step 1: Place your first slice of bread onto a clean plate.

Step 2: Semi-heavily butter the bread with the peanut butter, being sure to get it right up to the edge.

Step 3: Place a spoonful of jelly in the center of the bread.

WARNING: Make sure to not get the spoon contaminated with the peanut butter, or you might spread the peanut butter into the jelly jar.

Step 4: Place your second bread onto the peanut butter, with the jelly in the center.

Step 5: Press down on the center of the bread so the jelly spreads out to the edges.

Step 6: Once the jelly is sufficiently spread, you many then eat up!

Step 7: Enjoy!
